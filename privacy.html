Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Twenty47 Marketing ("Twenty47," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to twenty47marketing.com and the services we provide to our clients.

Information We Collect

We collect information you give us directly, including:

  • Contact & business details — name, business name, email address, phone number, industry, and website, submitted through our intake or booking forms.
  • Communications — messages you send us by form, email, phone, or text.
  • Usage data — basic, non-identifying analytics about how visitors use our site.

How We Use Your Information

  • To set up and deliver the services you purchase (including provisioning your CRM and sending account, onboarding, and support messages).
  • To respond to your inquiries and schedule calls.
  • To send account and onboarding notifications by email and, where you have opted in, by SMS.
  • To improve our website and services.

SMS / Text Messaging

If you provide your mobile number and check the consent box on our intake form, you agree to receive account and onboarding text messages from Twenty47 Marketing at the number provided (for example: a welcome/confirmation, login details, a setup walkthrough link, and a booking link).

  • Message frequency varies. Message & data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP at any time to opt out. Reply HELP for help, or email info@twenty47marketing.com.
  • Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of any purchase.

Mobile data is never sold or shared. Twenty47 Marketing does not sell or share your mobile opt-in information or SMS consent with any third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information is used solely to deliver the account and onboarding messages you signed up for. No mobile information will be shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers who help us operate (such as our CRM, email, and payment processors), and only as needed to deliver our services, or when required by law. As noted above, mobile opt-in data and SMS consent are never shared with third parties for marketing.

Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal obligations. You may request deletion of your information at any time (see Contact below).

Your Choices

  • Opt out of SMS by replying STOP.
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in any email.
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information by contacting us.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page, and we will update the "Last updated" date above.
